AI-Enhanced Apps For Marketing

These are some of the lightweight apps that I've created for my own work use. Unfortunately, these only exist as private apps (for now?), but they may provide valuable ideas you could steal.

If you have productivity ideas you'd like to build, I'd love to hear them. I might even be able to help you build them.

The first productivity app I made was my AI-powered news curator, which has its own dedicated page: Check out FeedSlasher.

A simplified explanation of what these apps do:

  1. SEO Pages Analysis - Immediately generates primary SEO topic, title/meta tags, and keywords for all webpages
  2. SEO Backlink Priorities - Creates a prioritized gameplan for getting backlinks from a set of domains
  3. Advanced Social Media Listener - Finds phrase mentions, then validates flagged opportunities with AI, and sends alerts to Slack

1. SEO: Pages Analysis

This tool analyzes the content of webpages, considers what in-market search traffic would be searching for, and creates:

  1. Instant generation of SEO title tags and meta descriptions for all pages
  2. Page-by-page SEO keyword/topic assessments
  3. A check on overall keyword coverage

The wild thing is... the results are really good. I give personal attention to page titles because those impact SEO rankings, but the AI creates some really good drafts. The meta descriptions, though...chef's kiss. They can be spot on.

How to use

There are other keyword ranking tools where you can say, "here are my keywords, how do we rank for them?" This tool is more helpful for planning. It says, "here are my pages, what do you think they're about?" Part of site planning is deciding which page will cover which SEO topic, and this let's me organize thoughts from that direction.

  • If the client is paying for SEO work: I can use this to look for gaps in SEO topics, or to identify content misalignment.
  • If a website client doesn't want to pay for SEO work, we can use this to create a quick draft of every page's SEO tags.

2. SEO: Backlink Priorities

This tool takes a list of domains and prioritizes them for link building efficiency and impact. I get:

  1. A prioritized list of websites to work on
  2. The type of website (directory, blog, etc.)
  3. A suggested link building strategy
  4. Other sortable domain attributes

The priority ranking is based on a formula that includes:

  1. Topical relevance to my website
  2. Domain authority
  3. Difficulty to get a link
  4. How many competitors also get links from this domain
  5. A custom modifier (minor impact)

That custom modifier can be anything I want. A common example would be to prioritize "easy to get" backlinks if SEO time is limited.

Where Do I Get My Domains List?

I can put any domains into the tool that I want. BUT what I typically do is use SEMRush to generate a report of domains that my top competitors get links from. I filter out the ones with very low domain authority. I leverage their work as my shortcut.

3. Advanced Social Media Listener

I have a couple of different versions of this tool for different social networks. Here's what it does:

  1. Queries a social media API to find posts with certain keywords or phrases, OR collects posts from subreddit RSS feeds
  2. Scores each post based on keywords and phrases
  3. Any posts above a score threshold are sent to AI for analysis
  4. If the AI determines the post actually matches what I wanted, I get an immediate slack alert

This could be configured for all sorts of social listening purposes. But the benefit here is that I don't have to scrub through posts based on keywords - the AI acts as a personal assistant to only flag what is worthy of my time.
