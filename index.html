About

I am the chief marketer on the Atmosphere Digital team. Atmosphere is a Webby award-winning agency with 20 years of experience. We work with clients of all sizes, from small non-profits and startups to Fortune 500 companies and federal government agencies. My previous experience includes 13 years as the Marketing VP for rapidly growing tech startups.

Outside of work, I regularly flex my creative side through music, DIY projects, and experimenting with new technologies. Lately, I'm enjoying building lightweight AI-powered apps for productivity, developing a SaaS side project, and playing with generative AI.
