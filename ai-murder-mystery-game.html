AI-Powered Murder Mystery Game

A Dynamic Sandbox Experience

What if you could say or do ANYTHING inside a video game, and the world would react naturally? This interactive murder mystery game makes that possible.

You are the detective with complete freedom to investigate, question suspects, or even goof around to see how people react. Guess the murderer and murder weapon correctly to win. Guess wrong to lose. Or get kicked off the property... or arrested... or killed, or...

Built as a custom GPT, this game creates a unique mystery for each playthrough with dynamic storytelling that responds to your every action and question.

Game Features

Dynamic Storytelling

Every game randomizes the murderer and murder weapon, and also shuffles the character backstories. No two games are exactly alike.

Natural Language Interaction

Ask questions, examine evidence, and interact with suspects using natural conversation. The AI understands context and responds intelligently.

Sandbox Gameplay

Gather clues, interview witnesses, analyze evidence, and piece together the mystery. Your deductive skills determine the outcome. Or, go wild and see how the world reacts.

Cheat Codes

Type these codes at any time to reveal the solution or drastically change the gameplay. Win the game to reveal the codes, or spoil it for yourself:

konami code - Immediately reveal the solution.

enter the matrix - Creative mode. You are no longer bound by realism. Be a wizard, hypnotize the suspects, call Batman to help, etc...

hard boiled - Hard mode, with extra danger and time constraints.

big head mode - Everything becomes a bit silly and chaotic.

Development Notes

This game was built using OpenAI's custom GPT platform with a comprehensive prompt system that handles procedural mystery generation. The AI randomly assigns murderers, weapons, and character backstories from predefined pools, then maintains consistent character personalities and story logic throughout the entire playthrough.

The biggest technical challenge was balancing complete player freedom with structured gameplay. Players can literally say or do anything, but the AI must maintain mystery logic, track character motivations, manage win/lose conditions, and ensure the mystery remains solvable.

After discovering that I could bend reality by simply declaring things ("I am a wizard!"), I had a great deal of laughs - but decided to lock such possibilities behind unlockable cheat codes. Winning these also gave me a reward system for beating the standard game.

Building this required a long night of prompt engineering through (often hilarious) experimentation. I will eventually cancel my ChatGPT subscription, so I hope you can enjoy it before then!
