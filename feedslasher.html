Cut Through The Noise

I don't know about you, but I am too busy to read news feeds.

Even with the best filtering, it's a chore.

Yet, I depend on catching timely news updates.

So, I built my own AI-powered news curator and summarization tool.

Forget keywords and filters. This is an AI assistant that knows YOU, WHAT you want to read, and WHY. Every day, you only receive the top news that aligns with your goals, along with AI summaries so you can act fast.

How It Works

Train Your AI Assistant

Define your role, what you're interested in, and how you will use the information. Pick your selection of news feeds for the AI to watch.

AI Curates The News

Knowing your needs, your AI assistant analyzes each day's articles, then selects the best for you.

AI Summarizes

Your best picks are summarized into easy-to-read bullet points. Optionally, it also highlights an action item based on your use case.

Get Your Digest

Receive a daily email with the top picks and their summaries. Just the best articles - quickly digestable.

Example Use Cases

A marketer wants to write shareworthy thought-leadership posts on LinkedIn. FeedSlasher selects timely news articles that are topical, inspire original commentary, prioritize viral potential, and that would be interesting to the marketer's target audiences.
A startup founder in AI healthcare wants to track early-stage funding and FDA policy shifts. FeedSlasher surfaces only the most relevant updates—competitor launches, investment moves, regulatory developments—so the founder stays ahead, spots opportunities early, and makes faster strategic decisions.
A policy researcher focused on digital privacy needs to track legislation, regulatory updates, and advocacy movements across jurisdictions. FeedSlasher pulls from a handpicked set of legal, governmental, and nonprofit feeds—surfacing only the updates that match their focus—so they can stay current, brief stakeholders, and respond quickly to emerging policy shifts.
