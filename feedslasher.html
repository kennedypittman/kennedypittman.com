Cut Through The Noise

I don't know about you, but I am too busy to read all the news each day. Even with the best filters, it's a chore.

Yet, I depend on catching timely news updates for my job.

So, I built my own AI-powered news curator to solve this. And it works great!

Imagine having an assistant that knows what you're after and WHY. Every day, it filters all your news feeds down to the few articles that align with your goals—and summarizes them so you can act fast.

Example Use Cases

A marketer wants to write shareworthy thought-leadership content on LinkedIn. FeedSlasher selects timely news articles that are topical, inspire original commentary, prioritize viral potential, and would be interesting to the marketer's target audiences.
A startup founder in AI healthcare wants to track early-stage funding and FDA policy shifts. FeedSlasher surfaces only the most relevant updates—competitor launches, investment moves, regulatory developments—so the founder stays ahead, spots opportunities early, and makes faster strategic decisions.
A policy researcher focused on digital privacy needs to track legislation, regulatory updates, and advocacy movements across jurisdictions. FeedSlasher pulls from a handpicked set of legal, governmental, and nonprofit feeds—surfacing only the updates that match their focus—so they can stay current, brief stakeholders, and respond quickly to emerging policy shifts.
