Codename: Project Wolf

A Scrapped Construction App

Many years ago, a company called Plangrid was the king of the mobile construction planning apps. Then they were acquired. They somewhat disappeared overnight as they were tucked into a much larger behemoth of an app ecosystem.

I briefly explored creating my own variation on the idea - mobile plans, synced across all users, and with added some features of my own. My design drafts received buy-in from my construction manager contacts, but I couldn't bring myself to invest funding or time into what was ultimately a copycat idea.
